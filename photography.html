Leif Tiltins - Burning Man 2014

It’s been a few years that I’ve wanted to go to Burning Man; this year I honestly felt that I had to. I left my camera in my tent almost the whole week once I realized just how small of a fragment of this experience a photo can actually capture. Maybe I’ll try again next year – this time was too precious to observe through a lens.
